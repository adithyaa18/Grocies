Places we find Fresh Vegetables

Evergreen Co-operative

Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England," is a city in Sri Lanka's central highlands with a cool, temperate climate ideal for growing a variety of vegetables. The region's fertile soil and consistent rainfall support the cultivation of carrots, leeks, cabbage, lettuce, broccoli, cauliflower, potatoes, beetroot, green beans, and peas. Farmers use a mix of traditional and modern sustainable practices, including organic fertilizers and crop rotation, to maintain high-quality produce.

Green farms Gampaha

Green Farms in Gampaha is a company that producing vegetables organically and using a environmentally friendly methods. Currently the farm specializes in vegetables where environment and soil of Gampaha help to select the following; green leafy vegetables such as spinach, lettuce and kangkung; gourds like bitter gourd and snake gourd; nightshades like eggplant, tomatoes and chili; root and tuber crops such as sweet potatoes yams and carrots; legumes and pods such as long beans and green peas; and herbs like coriander,

Sunshine co-operative

Sunshine Co-operative in Gampaha is well recognized successful and quality producing vegetable farming company by using natural and sustainable way due to the climatic conditions with tropical and adequate fertility. They grow different classes of vegetables such as leafy vegetables which include spinach, lettuce, kangkung, gourds which includes bitter gourd, snake gourd, nightshade which includes eggplants, tomatoes, chilly, roots and tubers such as sweet potatoes yams, carrots, legumes such as long beans, green peas and herbs such as coriander, mint and curry leaves.
